> Status: FOUNDATION LOCKED — additive implementation only.

## Purpose

The Sovereign Product Reality Engine (SPRE) is the product meta-system above NEXARA Design OS. It maintains a governed, evidence-backed digital twin of product intent, experience semantics, implementation, runtime truth, human control, telemetry, and controlled evolution.

NEXARA Design OS remains a subsystem. Figma, SwiftUI, Web, Canva, and future visionOS experiences are projections of one product reality, not independent sources of truth.

## Non-negotiable invariants

1. Mission First.
2. Human Sovereignty remains visible for consequential actions.
3. Runtime Truth cannot be replaced by optimistic UI state.
4. Evidence is required for verified completion.
5. Provider identity is not product identity.
6. Evolution is proposed, simulated, verified, approved when required, promoted, monitored, and reversible.
7. No private chain-of-thought is exposed; only concise rationale, assumptions, policy decisions, and evidence.
8. Existing NEXARA L01–L12 architecture remains fixed; SPRE is a cross-layer product meta-system.
